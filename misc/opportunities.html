Technology Opportunities

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has opened up many ways to enhance student education. Some of the opportunities include but are not limited to:

• Personalized learning paths: Leveraging AI to cater to individual students' strengths, weaknesses, and learning styles, educational institutions can provide targeted support in areas where students require assistance. For instance, the University of Murcia in Spain conducted a test with an AI-enabled chatbot, designed to address students' questions about the campus and areas of study. The results were remarkable, as the chatbot successfully responded to over 38,708 questions with an impressive accuracy rate of over 91%, showcasing AI's ability to guide students effectively.
(How AI and Data Could Personalize Higher Education, 2019)

• Intelligent tutoring: using real-time feedback to identify where AI detects where a student is struggling and offers assistance to problem areas leading to better marks. The learning model is founded on the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) which evaluates the personalities of individuals including their qualities and preferences regarding how they learn and adjust feedback based on it.
(Kumar, 2023)

• Enhanced Content Creation: AI algorithms can generate educational content such as quizzes, practice exercises and lesson plans. This saves educators time and effort, allowing them to focus on more interactive and engaging teaching activities.

• Accessibility and Inclusivity: AI can support learners with disabilities through the use of speech recognition, text-to-speech and image recognition. This reflects a commitment towards inclusivity and equality among all learners.

• Administrative Support: AI systems can streamline administrative tasks, such as grading assignments, scheduling, and record-keeping, freeing up time for educators to focus on teaching and providing individualized support to students.
