Technology Choices

It is crucial to evaluate the choices available before adopting AI technology or thinking about its applications. Here are critical assessment of some key points to consider:

•Benefits vs. Risks

Although AI offers various advantages such as automation, efficiency, and data analysis ability, it is necessary to consider whether using AI for education is more risky than the benefits because there are risks such as privacy issues, biased decisions, overreliance on technology, and AI misinformation. Is an enhanced learning experience worth the risk of data privacy?

•Teacher-student relationship

Although AI can improve learning, it shouldn't take the place of teachers or human interaction in the classroom. The decisions made when implementing AI should put maintaining the teacher-student relationship first and make sure that AI serves as a supportive tool rather than a replacement for human direction.

Because AI can sometimes produce unequal outcomes for students and it can also lead to a decrease in the sense of connection with humans, as there is no interaction with teachers.
